Privacy Policy


We Collect No Personal Information From Users


At this time, we do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data when you use StickARs for iOS.

All placed stickers are stored locally and available only to you. We never see their location or customizations.

When you scan the world and place stickers, location data is stored in the world map saved locally on your device, but we have no access to that data and it is not shared with anyone else.
